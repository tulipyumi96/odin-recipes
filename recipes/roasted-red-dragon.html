Roasted Red Dragon

This recipe cleverly uses the undying fire and steel-like skin of the Red Dragon. Dragon meat is tough, but this recipe brings out its unmatched flavor.

Ingredients

  • A block of Red Dragon meat.....1 kg
  • Wine.....50 ml
  • Salt & Pepper.....pinch

Directions

  1. Prepare a piece of Dragon skin with the scales still attached.
  2. Heat up the scales by placing it in the undying fire of the Dragon.
  3. Once the scales are sizzling hot, take the block of Dragon meat and sear all of the edges until golden.
  4. While searing, mix together the wine, salt & pepper in a pot and bring to a simmer.
  5. Once the sides of the meat are nice and seared, place the block into the pot with the sauce. Make sure that the whole block is covered in the sauce.
  6. Take the pot off of the heat and let the meat marinate for 30 min.
  7. Once marinated, slice the block of meat into thin slices and serve.
  8. Enjoy!
